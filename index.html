Welcome to MDT E-Licensing

Licensing by MDT ensure all businesses follow local laws and operate safely.It includes licenses for shops,vendors,hawkers, and signage.

New License Application

Users can apply for various business licenses online without needing to visit the office.

License Renewal

Easily renew existing licenses before they expire through a simple online process.

Online Payment

Pay license fees securely through the online payment system.

Document Upload & Download

Upload required documents and download approval letters,forms, or invoices.

Benefits of E-Licensing

E-Licensing allows businesses to apply online,save time,reduce paperwork, and access services anytime,anywhere.It also simplifies renewals and improves user convenience.
