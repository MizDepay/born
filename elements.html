We strive to provide friendly service to customers and help resolve issues related to licence/permit applications and approvals.

Customer Charter

No. Type of License Customer Charter
1. Business Without Risk Immediately with complete documentation and meeting the processing requirements, it usually takes 24 hours.
2. Risky Business Immediately with the condition that the application and department review are complete,it usually takes 30 days. If incomplete, it will be brought to the meeting for consideration of a temporary license.
3. Advertising License The license will be issued immediately, subject to the guidelines' conditions.
4 Vendor License
Mobile Vendors – Immediately with Location Requirements

Permanent Vendors – Immediately with the Condition of Complete Application with Review from the Health Office and District Land Office.
5 Temporary License Outside Premises 1 Day
6 Night Market License 1 Day
7 Banner Permit Immediately with conditions according to the guidelines.
8 Sidewalk Vendor Permit Immediately
9 Seasonal Fruit Permit 1 Day

Enforcement Division

1.We guarantee the welfare of the residents and resolve their issues based on the applicable laws through swift, efficient, and people-friendly enforcement actions.

2. Ready to hear public appeals regarding every offence committed and set fair and just fines.

3. Ready to receive public complaints and conduct investigations promptly within 14 days.
