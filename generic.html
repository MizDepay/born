Complete Business License Application Process

The licensing process is a structured procedure that businesses must follow to legally operate within the Tangkak District. It ensures that all business activities comply with local laws, safety standards, and zoning regulations. The process involves registering or logging in to the system, submitting complete business information, attaching the required documents, undergoing a review by the licensing department, and making payment before receiving an official license. This process helps maintain public order, safety, and supports economic growth in the district.

1. Register / Log In

Users must create an account or log in to the e-Licensing platform using their valid credentials. This helps secure user data and allows the system to track and manage individual license applications.

2.Fill in Application Form

Once logged in, applicants are required to fill out an online form with complete business information such as business name, type of business activity, premise address, and contact details. Choosing the correct license category ensures proper processing.

3.Upload Required Documents

Applicants must upload supporting documents such as a copy of the identity card (IC), Companies Commission of Malaysia (SSM) registration certificate, photos of the business premises, and other relevant files depending on the type of business.

4. Submit Application

After completing the form and uploading all documents, applicants must review the information for accuracy and click the submit button. This sends the application to the licensing department for further action.

5.Application Review

The Majlis Daerah Tangkak (MDT) will review the application and verify all submitted documents. In some cases, officers may conduct a site inspection to ensure the business premises comply with safety and zoning regulations before approval.

6.Make Payment

Once the application is approved, applicants will receive a notification to make payment. Payment can be done online through the system’s integrated payment gateway, making the process faster and more convenient.

7.Download License

After successful payment, the applicant can download and print the official business license from their account. This license serves as legal proof of permission to operate and must be displayed at the business premises.

Type Of License

This the Explainantion of each type of business and you can choose what type are license you want to request.

1.Business Premises License & Advertising

A license required for operating a business at a physical location and placing business signage or advertisements.

2.Advertising License

Approval needed to display promotional banners, buntings, signboards, or any commercial advertisement in public areas.

3.Hawker License

License granted to individuals selling goods or food from mobile stalls or pushcarts at approved locations.

4.Dog License

A permit issued to pet owners to legally own and register their dogs with the local authority.

5.Night Market / Lambak License

Authorization to run a stall or booth in night markets or “pasar lambak” under council-regulated areas and times.

6.Permit to Install Banner & Banting

Permission to set up temporary shelters, canopies, or display buntings for business or events.

7.Permit to Place Business Goods on Five-Foot Way

Permit for placing goods, items, or displays in front of business premises on the pedestrian walkway.

8.Permit to Sell Seasonal Fruits.

Short-term license for selling fruits like durian, rambutan, or mangoes during specific fruiting seasons.

9.Temporary Permit

A short-term license for running a business or setting up a stall for a limited period, such as during festivals or events.

10.Permit to Place Chairs and Tables Outside Premises

Approval for restaurants or eateries to place dining tables and chairs on sidewalks or outdoor areas.

11.Cancellation of Advertising / Hawker Business License

A process for businesses to officially cancel or terminate their existing advertising or hawker license.

12. Return Trust Money

A formal application to claim back deposit money (if any) held by the local council after license expiry or cancellation.
